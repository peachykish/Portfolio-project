About Me

Hey y’all! I’m a former teacher turned UX Designer with 7 years in K-12 education.
Teachers design experiences for our students multiple times a day in multiple ways. We plan units that are very similar to design sprints and even have meetings multiple times a week that are comparable to retrospectives. The ways that we track data are relatable to the ways one interacts with SCRUM boards. So one might say that I have quite a lot of experience in UX Design that will translate seamlessly into a role as a UX Designer and positively impact any team of designers I work with.
I am empathetic, which leads me to constantly put myself into the shoes of the user(s). I am passionate about using my skills to amplify equitable access. I am an observant person and I’ve learned to use my observations to help me create effective solutions. My years in the classroom molded me into a flexible peacemaker with the skills to encourage my team members to stay on task. I am self reflective and consistent at naming areas where I glow and areas where I still need to grow. I am mindful of efficiency and focused on creating useful MVPs that are easy for users to interact with.
